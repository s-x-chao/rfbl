2023 Top 100 Prospects

With all full-season levels of the minor leagues starting, it’s time to update our ranking of the Top 100 prospects.

Since our initial Top 100 for 2023 published in January, there have been prospects whose rankings we have adjusted due to injuries, new information and updated reports from spring training, as well as graduations from early in the major league season.

The most notable addition is Padres catcher Ethan Salas, who signed out of Venezuela for $5.6 million on Jan. 15. After drawing plenty of attention from international scouts as an amateur, Salas has continued to dazzle pro scouts for his mix of hitting ability and defense, doing so against significantly older competition. He joins Rays shortstop Wander Franco and Yankees outfielder Jasson Dominguez as international signings who ranked in the Top 100 before they made their official pro debuts.
